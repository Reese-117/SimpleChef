# Instructions: 
Each team will have 10 minutes to present their project (including a short demo video), followed by a 2-minute Q&A session. Your presentation should be clear, concise, and well-structured. Ensure you manage your time effectively to cover all key aspects of your project.
Note: Instead of running your project live during the presentation, you are required to include a short demo video as part of your 10-minute slot. Make sure your video is ready and embedded in your slides ahead of time.
Your presentation should contain the following:
1. Introduction (state your problem and why it matters),
2. Methodology (explain your approach, tools, and design decisions),
3. Implementation (walk through the technical aspects of your UI, challenges faced, and standout features), and
4. Demo (show a recorded walkthrough of your UI highlighting key functionalities and user interaction).

Presentation Rubrics (10 pts)
1. Introduction (2)
2. Methodology (2)
3. Implementation (2)
4. Demo (2)
5. Q&A (2)
